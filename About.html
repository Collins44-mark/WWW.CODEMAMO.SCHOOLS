Studies Available

We offer a well-rounded curriculum designed to prepare students for the future. Subjects include:

  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Language Arts (English, Literature)
  • Social Studies
  • Computer Science
  • Physical Education

Our aim is to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills in students from a young age.

Our Facilities

We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art facilities which include:

  • Modern Science Laboratories
  • Fully equipped Computer Lab
  • Extensive Library
  • Sports Grounds and Gymnasium
  • Music and Art Studios

Our students have access to top-tier resources to enhance both their academic and extracurricular growth.

Sports Activities

Physical fitness is a crucial part of our students' development. We offer a variety of sports, including:

  • Football
  • Basketball
  • Swimming
  • Track and Field
  • Table Tennis

Our students engage in both competitive sports and recreational activities to maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Fees Structure

We offer affordable and transparent pricing. Below is the breakdown of the annual school fees:

  • Nursery: 1,200,000 per year
  • Class 1 - Class 3: 1,500,000 per year
  • Class 4 - Class 6: 2,100,000 per year
  • Class 7: 2,400,000 per year

Payments are due in two installments: the first in January, and the second in June. Discounts are available for early payments and siblings.
